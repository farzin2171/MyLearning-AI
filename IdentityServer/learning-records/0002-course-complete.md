# Full 6-lesson course built; team onboarding curriculum complete

All six lessons and two reference documents have been produced for the Equisoft identity onboarding course. The curriculum covers the complete arc a new developer needs: ecosystem orientation → OAuth flows → token anatomy → Authorization Service internals → client/API registration → building a new service end-to-end.

**Implications:**
- Next sessions should focus on depth, not breadth — pick one lesson and probe harder (e.g. SAML federation, multi-tenant secrets management, adding a new external role provider)
- A good next exercise is to have a real new developer follow Lesson 6 against an actual new service and report back where they got stuck — that feedback drives lesson refinements
- The Authorization Service cheat sheet (`reference/authorization-cheatsheet.html`) is ready for day-1 printing
